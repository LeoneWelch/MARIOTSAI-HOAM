MARIO TSAI Studio
Founded in Hangzhou in the summer of 2014, Mario Tsai Studio is a research-based practice that pride itself on innovative thinking to produce sustainable design. Unconstrained by conventional methods, in whatever industry or culture it works in, the studio uses rigorous logic coupled with a fluid sense of artistry to present the works, whether in the form of products, installations, strategies or exhibitions.

Founded in Hangzhou in the summer of 2014, Mario Tsai Studio is a research-based practice that pride itself on innovative thinking to produce sustainable design. Unconstrained by conventional methods, in whatever industry or culture it works in, the studio uses rigorous logic coupled with a fluid sense of artistry to present the works, whether in the form of products, installations, strategies or exhibitions.
In recent years, Mario Tsai Studio has been dedicated to self-driven design research, exploring the origin and logic of things, and combing and designing the creation process with macro thinking. The studio is dedicated to the design variables brought by technological innovation, unconventional use of materials and structural innovation, with ductility and longevity as the basic principles for its designs.

Eponymous brand MARIO TSAI
MARIO TSAI, the independent brand of Mario Tsai Studio, is likewise guided by experimental, flexible thinking, focusing attention on a project’s material characteristics, systematic structure and the sustainability of its life cycle, and integrating into it a soft minimalist aesthetic.


Collaborations
The design collaborations of Mario Tsai Studio are not only limited to product design, but also aims to but also aims to export solutions with advanced design thinking to clients from different fields with an open mind. With an international perspective and solid professional skills, Mario Tsai Studio provides consulting services in product design, art installation and event planning, as well as brand strategy and art direction for brands.


Founder Mario Tsai
Two years of practical experience in design and production in a Beijing furniture factory after graduation in 2011 has honed Mario's solid professional skills and macro thinking.Since the establishment of his studio in 2014, Mario has always maintained his independent thinking and passion for design, exploring a unique and suitable design development path through his continuous design practice and global exhibition experience, moving forward with courage and restraint.

合作
Mario Tsai Studio的设计合作不仅限于产品设计，还以开放的心态向各领域的客户提供先进的设计思维解决方案。凭借国际视野和扎实的专业技术，Mario Tsai Studio提供产品设计、艺术装置和活动策划以及品牌战略和艺术指导咨询服务。


创始人马里奥蔡
2011年毕业后，在北京家具厂的两年设计和生产实践经验中，Mario磨练了扎实的专业技术和宏观思维。自2014年成立工作室以来，Mario始终保持独立的设计思维和热情。通过持续的设计实践和全球展览经验，提出独特而适合的设计发展路径，并以勇气和克制向前迈进。
